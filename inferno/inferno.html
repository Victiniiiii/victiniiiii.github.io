Questions and Answers
Is the Inferno minion the best minion ?
It depends on the minion level, but usually it is better than most of the minions.
Why is Gabagool the Fish not included in calculations ?
The auction prices in Skyblock API is really broken, also auction items are a lot easier to manipulate.
Is it worth to switch to inferno minions ?
Tier 2 Inferno minions are one of the cheapest and the most profitable setups.
What is the best fuel for the Inferno minions ?
The best fuel is tier 1 gabagool, it will be tier 2 when Derpy is mayor, and tier 3 if Derpy is mayor and the minions are really high leveled.
Why are some items toggled on in the bazaar settings menu ?
I have hand selected the ones that are profitable to sell order, and sell decently quickly.
Does the calculator include the double Inferno Apex drop perk ?
Yes, it does include Ionized Ceramic prices too, however i didn't include super compactors as it wouldn't affect the output much.
