Frequently Asked Questions
What is the Composter ?

The Composter is a feature in Hypixel Skyblock that allows the player to create Compost.
The Composter takes 10 minutes to create each Compost, consuming 4000 Organic Matter and 2000 Machine Fuel in the process.
However, these values can be improved by upgrading the Composter.

Why do people want more Compost ?

Compost can be exchanged for money in the bazaar, thus creating passive income for the Composter owner.
