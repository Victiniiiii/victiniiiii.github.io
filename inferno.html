Questions and Answers
Is the Inferno minion the best minion ?
It depends on the minion level, but usually better than most of the minions.
Why is Gabagool the Fish not included in calculations ?
The auction prices in Skyblock API is really broken, also auction items are a lot easier to manipulate the price.
Is it worth to switch to infernos ?
Tier 2 Infernos are one of the cheapest and the most profitable setups.
What is the best fuel for the Inferno minions ?
The best fuel is usually tier 1 gabagool, it can be tier 2 while Derpy is mayor, and tier 3 if the minions are really high leveled.
Why are some items toggled on in the bazaar settings menu ?
I have hand selected the ones that are profitable to sell order, and sell decently quickly.
Does the calculator include the double Inferno Apex drop perk ?
Yes, it does include Ionized Ceramic prices too, however i didn't include super compacotrs as it wouldn't affect the output much.
